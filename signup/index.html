Join the Music Tutors of Sydney

Are you an experienced music tutor looking to meet students without the hassle of music schools and tutoring agencies? Well you've come to the right place! MToS is dedicated to a practice called horizontal organization; a stlye of organization that places all members on equal footing and ensures that everyone is seen, heard, and above all, gets a fair go. We believe that the only thing that matters in tuition is you and your students, and we're here to make sure both can teach, learn and work in a safe and sustainable way.

Music Tutors of Sydney is a fledgling organization with big plans. We aspire to be to offer all the guidance, marketing and support our members need, as well as be a fun and social community where musicians can meet, hang out, collaborate and share their experience.
