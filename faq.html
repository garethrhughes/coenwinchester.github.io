Frequently Asked Questions

← Home
What equipment do I need?

You’ll need a guitar (acoustic or electric) and a pick. If you don’t own one yet, we can discuss rental options during the first lesson.

How long does it take to learn a song?

It varies. With weekly 60‑minute lessons most beginners can play a simple three‑chord song within 4–6 weeks.

Do you teach music theory?

Yes! Theory is woven into each lesson, from basic chord construction to advanced improvisation concepts.

Can I bring a friend to a trial lesson?

Absolutely—first lessons are free and you’re welcome to bring a friend along.
