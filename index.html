All Music, No Hassle

Regardless of age, skill, experience or style, the best way to reach your goals is with an experienced and professional tutor. And the best way to find the right tutor for you is with Music Tutors of Sydney. MToS is a musician's collective, meaning no admin fees, no signup and no fuss. All you need to do is reach out to any of our friendly tutors, or submit an enquiry and we'll find who's right for you.

Music Tutors of Sydney is a non-profit service that respects music tutelage as a time-honoured tradition for experts to pass on their skills and wisdom to aspiring musicians, regardless of whether those skills will be shared around a campfire, from the stadium stage or even just enjoyed in your spare time. With MToS 100% of your fees go to your tutor, ensuring that they can continue doing what they do best; helping you reach your goals and continuing the rich tradition of music tutelege.
